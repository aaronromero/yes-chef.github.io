San Tung Dry Fried Chicken

Description

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Wings Ingredients:

  • Chicken wings or bonelss chicken breast diced into 1 inch cubes
  • salt
  • 0.33 cup mochiko - sweet glutinous rice flour
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • oil for frying

Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1.25 cup water
  • 5-10 slices of ginger
  • 1 glove garlic finley minced
  • 0.5 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 0.25 tsp white pepper
  • 0.25-0.5 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp corn syrup
  • 1.5 tbsp honey

Sauce:

  1. Mix everything except for the corn syrup and honey in a small sauce pan, place over medium heat, stir frequently, when it starts to boil add in corn syrup and honey. Add more soy sauce if its too sweet, or even water.
  2. Suggest to make the sauce the night before and refrigerate. The sauce will thicken overnight.

Wings:

  1. Heat oil in a pan until 350 degrees.
  2. In a shallow dish, mix the flours together, sprinkle salt and pepper on chicken, and lightly coat the wings.
  3. Drop in the wings into the hot oil, and cook for 5 minutes, remove the chicken from the pan, and dab on a paper towel, let cool for a few minutes.
  4. Place the wings back into the hot oil, and fry for another 5 minutes. Remove and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
